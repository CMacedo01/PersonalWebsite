Community Service

Trunk or Treat

A fellow organization officer and I managed a team of over 20 students, who were also part of our organization, at a community Halloween event. Students were tasked with managing activity tents and maintaining the festive spirit as hundreds of children came over to enjoy treats and Halloween shows.

Art Installation

Members of our organization helped a local art institution move and install various art pieces for an upcoming show. A small team of 12 students managed by myself worked cautiously and efficiently to have the installation in working order within the day.

Houston Toy Drive

In preparation for Christmas, our organization partnered with the local fire department and many other local organizations to ensure that children of all ages had a gift for the holidays. With over 500 children in attendance, members had their hands full all day while still maintaining a cheerful spirit.
